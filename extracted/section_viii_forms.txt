Schedules in accordance with ITB Clause 13 with the requirements of the

accepted Bid resulting from price corrections when it is finalized at the time of
contract award. The Price Schedule and Schedule of Requirements which form
part of the contract should be modified accordingly.

accordance with ITB Clause 4.2. Failure to submit it with the Bid shall result
in the rejection of the Bid and the Bidder’s disqualification.
The Performance Securing Declaration, if allowed and Bank Guarantee
Form for Advance Payment shall be completed only by the successful Bidder
in accordance with one of the forms indicated herein by the Procuring Entity,
and pursuant to GCC Clause 13 and its corresponding SCC provision.
